## Design Context

### Users
Solo founders and first-time entrepreneurs launching US startups. They're often overwhelmed by legal/financial complexity and need a tool that feels authoritative yet calming. Used on desktop and mobile, often during evenings/weekends. The app guides them through incorporation, legal docs, fundraising, and hiring AI agents.

### Brand Personality
**Minimal, refined, elegant.** Like a well-designed financial instrument — every element earns its place. Reduces cognitive load. Inspires confidence that you're doing things right.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light with cool tones. Cool white backgrounds, slate neutrals, subtle indigo accent.
- **Typography**: Distinctive sans-serif (not Inter/Roboto). Clean, modern, with strong hierarchy.
- **Color**: Slate neutral palette with a single indigo accent used sparingly for CTAs, progress, active states. Neutrals are dominant.
- **Radius**: Subtle (4-6px). Gentle softening, still structured and precise.
- **Shadows**: Minimal. Clean borders preferred over shadows. Use shadow only for elevation (modals, dropdowns).
- **Spacing**: Generous. Content breathes. Varied rhythm — not the same padding everywhere.
- **References**: impeccable.style (intentional, purposeful design)
- **Anti-references**: Generic SaaS dashboards, AI-generated card grids, glassmorphism, gradient text

### Design Principles
1. **Every element earns its place** — No decorative filler. If it doesn't help the user, remove it.
2. **Hierarchy through contrast, not clutter** — Size, weight, and color do the work. Don't add borders/boxes when spacing alone creates structure.
3. **Calm confidence** — The interface should feel like a trusted advisor. No urgency theater, no flashy animations. Steady, clear, reliable.
4. **Progressive disclosure** — Show one thing at a time. Complexity exists but is revealed through interaction, not dumped on screen.
5. **Intentional variety** — Break monotony. Not every section needs the same layout. Asymmetry and rhythm over rigid grids.
